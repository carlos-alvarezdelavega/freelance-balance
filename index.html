Freelance Balance

This is a study aimed at understanding work-life balance of freelancers who use the platform Upwork. In this website, you will find all the necessary information about the study and how to participate.

Who can take part?

We are recruiting professionals that use the Upwork platform regularly. To take part you must fulfill the following requirements:



  • Be 18 years or older.
  • Use the platform Upwork regularly (in the past six months or more) to conduct freelance work and have successfully completed at least one job through the platform.
  • Intend to use the Upwork platform as a freelancer during the period of the study (upcoming months).

    • About the study

    We will ask you to take part in a 12-day study with pre- and post-interviews. During the 12-day study you will complete three short daily activities that involve:



  • Reflecting on your workday.
  • Sharing if you feel Upwork disrupts aspects of your day.
  • Express how in control of your felt of your work-life balance each day.


    • You will use your smartphone to complete these activities through WhatsAppIn detail, the study will consist of the four stages below.



    1. Registration Survey

    Fill in a short survey with all some information about you and your work. The survey shouldnâ€™t take more than 15 mins to complete. All information will be kept confidential.

    2. On-Boarding Interview

    This interview is aimed at explaining in detail the activities you will complete during the study. The lead researcher will reiterate the purposes of the study and clarify any questions you might have. This interview will last between 15-30 minutes and will take place over Microsoft Teams or Skype at a time of your convenience.

    3. Work-Life Balance Activities

    Starting the day after the on-boarding interview, you will be asked to complete a series of short, daily activities for 12 days, which should not take you more than 10 minutes. The activities will consist of sharing your views of three aspects of your day through WhatsApp. You can respond to these activities using different formats, such as taking photos, recording voice notes, filming yourself, or simply writing down your answers. You will receive daily reminders via WhatsApp with the activity prompts; you should complete these activities whenever is most convenient to you, and not when you get the reminders.

    * If you do not have access to WhatsApp, you can still take part in the study by sharing your activities via email.

    4. Follow-up Interview

    After completing the work-life balance activities, you will take part in a follow-up interview within a week from the end of the activities. The purpose of this interview is to talk through your experience with the study, provide more details, and more contextual explanations. The interview will last between 60-90 minutes and will take place over Microsoft Teams or Skype at a date and time of your convenience.
